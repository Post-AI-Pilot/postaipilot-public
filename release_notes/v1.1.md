# Post AI Pilot v1.1 — Release Notes

**Date:** 29 March 2026  

---

## Highlights

v1.1 adds a professional product studio, AI influencer system, credit-based flexible usage for video and image generation, performance reporting, and carousel publishing support to Post AI Pilot. The content planner and subscription packages remain the core of the platform — this release builds new layers on top of them.

---

## 🎯 Credit System — Flexible Usage Model

Photo and video generations now can be run on a credit-based system. Subscription packages include defined credit amounts; users who need more can purchase additional credits via iyzico on a pay-as-you-go basis.

**What changed:**
- Generations run on an independent credit balance — planner subscription and credit balance are separate
- Different credit amounts defined per subscription tier
- Real-time balance tracking with automatic redirect on insufficient credits
- Credit cost varies by output type (photo vs. video, cost scales with video duration)
- Video extension lengths: 15, 22, 29, and 36 seconds — each tier priced accordingly

**Why it matters:** Users can leverage their creativity without being locked into rigid package limits. Low entry barrier for SMBs, no ceiling for power users.

## 📸 AI Product Studio

A full-featured studio interface that transforms product photos into professional e-commerce visuals and video ads.

**Flow:**
1. **Upload** — Drag-and-drop product photo upload (JPG, PNG, WebP, max 10 MB)
2. **AI Analysis** — Automatic product analysis: lighting quality, angle suitability, resolution score. Images scoring below 70/100 are rejected
3. **Select Concept** — AI-suggested scene concepts tailored to the product, or free-form prompt writing (pre-built chips available: marble surface, tropical leaves, neon lights, etc.)
4. **Select Platform** — Platform-specific optimization: Hepsiburada, Trendyol, Amazon (e-commerce) or Instagram, Facebook, TikTok (social media)
5. **Configure Output** — Photo or video ad selection, aspect ratio (1:1, 3:2, 2:3), video duration 4-8 seconds
6. **Generate** — Background removal → prompt optimization → scene rendering → final touches. Photo ~1-3 min, video ~2-4 min

## 🎬 Video Extension

Users can now extend AI-generated videos beyond the initial output, enabling longer-form content from a single generation.

**Available extension lengths:**
- 15 seconds
- 22 seconds
- 29 seconds
- 36 seconds

**Details:**
- Extension credit cost scales with the selected duration
- Extensions are applied to existing Veo-generated video outputs
- Extended videos are downloadable in HD alongside the original

**Why it matters:** Brands that need longer content for TikTok, Instagram Reels, or YouTube Shorts can now extend a single high-quality generation instead of re-prompting from scratch — saving both time and credits.

## 🤖 AI Influencer(Beta)

A system for creating and managing brand-specific AI influencer characters.

**Features:**
- Character design by selecting physical attributes: gender, age range, eye color, hair color
- Face image and full-body image generation
- Approval mechanism: created influencers go through a review process
- Dashboard: total influencer count, approved and pending characters
- Created influencers can be used directly in social media content

**Why it matters:** No other SMB-focused tool in Turkey offers this as an in-platform feature. Brands create their own digital representatives without paying real influencer costs.
